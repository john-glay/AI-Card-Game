Bato Bato Pick Card Game


Rules of the Game


Setup:

Each player is equipped with a carefully constructed deck consisting of 5 Rock cards (4 base damage), 5 Scissors cards (5 base damage), and 5 Paper cards (3 base damage). To enhance gameplay strategy, each deck also includes special Power-Up cards: 3 Fire Power-Ups, which add 2 bonus damage to a played card; 3 Thunder Power-Ups, which stun the opponent and prevent them from using any Power-Up cards in the next round; and 3 Water Power-Ups, which neutralize the effects of Fire Power-Ups and reduce incoming damage by 1. At the beginning of the game, each player's deck is shuffled, and they draw 5 random cards to form their starting hand. Each player starts with 30 health points, and the goal is to use a combination of base cards and Power-Ups to strategically attack the opponent and reduce their health to zero while managing one's own health and resources effectively.


Turn Phase:

  1. Card Selection
    • Players will choose 1 Base Card ( rock, paper, scissors ) and may attach 1 Power-Up.
    • The card will be facedown and revealed simultaneously.
  2. Power-Up Effects
    • Fire [ + 2 damage, but can be cancelled by water ].
    • Thunder [ If your card wins, your opponent can't use power-ups next turn ].
    • Water [ cancels fire and reduces damage by 1 ].
  3. Determine Round Winner
    • Typical Rock, Paper, Scissors rules/logic.
    • The winning card deals base damage + power-ups [ adjusted when water power-ups are used ].
    • The loser will take the damage and have their health points reduced.
  4. Draw Phase
    • Each player draws 1 card to replenish their hand back up to 5.
  5. Repeat until one player reaches zero health points.
